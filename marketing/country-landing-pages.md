### COMMON MISTAKES (Nigerians Make)

## Avoid These UK Visa Rejection Triggers

**❌ Showing lump sum deposits**
Visa officers look for consistency. Money that appears suddenly = borrowed funds.
**Fix:** Show 28 days of stable balance history.

**❌ Using distant relatives as sponsors**
Uncles, cousins, family friends = high rejection risk.
**Fix:** Use immediate family (parents, siblings) only.

**❌ Wrong bank account type**
Domiciliary accounts can raise questions.
**Fix:** Use current or savings accounts with your name.

**❌ Missing the 28-day rule**
Funds must sit in your account for exactly 28 days before you apply.
**Fix:** Check dates carefully. Day 29 = safe to apply.

**❌ Forgetting TB test**
You MUST test at an approved clinic in Lagos, Abuja, or Ibadan.
**Fix:** Book early. Certificate valid for 6 months.
